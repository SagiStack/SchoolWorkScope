Grown Ups

2010 • Comedy • Dir. Dennis Dugan

Plot Summary

After their high school basketball coach passes away, five good friends and former teammates reunite for a Fourth of July holiday weekend. They decide to spend the holiday weekend with their families at a lake house where they had celebrated their championship years prior.

Picking up where they left off, they discover why growing older doesn't mean growing up. The weekend becomes a hilarious journey of reconnecting, reliving their youth, and showing their kids how to have good old-fashioned fun.
