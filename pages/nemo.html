Finding Nemo

2003 • Animation / Adventure • Dir. Andrew Stanton

Plot Summary

Marlin, an overprotective clownfish, embarks on a perilous journey across the ocean to find his captured son, Nemo, joined by the forgetful Dory.

After his son is captured in the Great Barrier Reef and taken to Sydney, a timid clownfish named Marlin sets out on a journey to bring him home. He is joined by a friendly but forgetful blue tang fish named Dory. Together, they encounter vegetarian sharks, surfer dude turtles, hypnotic jellyfish, and hungry seagulls. Meanwhile, Nemo plots his escape from a dentist's fish tank with the help of the "Tank Gang".
