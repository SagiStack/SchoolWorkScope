The Dictator

2012 • Comedy • Dir. Larry Charles

Plot Summary

The heroic story of Admiral General Haffaz Aladeen, the tyrannical and narcissistic dictator of the fictional North African Republic of Wadiya. Aladeen rules with an iron fist, surrounds himself with female bodyguards, and risks his life to ensure that democracy would never come to the country he so lovingly oppressed.

When he travels to New York to address the UN, he is stripped of his power and forced to live as an ordinary person in the city, where he discovers a new side of life and love, all while plotting to regain his throne.
