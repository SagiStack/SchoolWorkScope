White Chicks

2004 • Comedy / Crime • Dir. Keenen Ivory Wayans

Plot Summary

Two disgraced FBI agent brothers, Kevin and Marcus Copeland, go way undercover in an effort to protect hotel heiresses the Wilson Sisters from a kidnapping plot. When the sisters suffer minor facial cuts and refuse to be seen in public, the brothers devise an outrageous plan.

They disguise themselves as the white socialites using elaborate prosthetics and makeup, and attend the high-profile Hamptons weekend event in their place. While undercover, they struggle to fit into high society, dealing with rival socialites and jealous boyfriends.
