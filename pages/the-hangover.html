The Hangover

2009 • Comedy • Dir. Todd Phillips

Plot Summary

Two days before his wedding, Doug Billings travels to Las Vegas with his best friends Phil and Stu and his future brother-in-law Alan for a bachelor party they promise they'll never forget. However, when the three groomsmen wake up the next morning, they can't remember a thing.

With a tiger in the bathroom, a baby in the closet, and the groom missing, they must retrace their bad decisions from the night before to figure out what went wrong and find Doug in time to get him back to Los Angeles for his wedding.
