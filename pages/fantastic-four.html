Fantastic Four: First Steps

2025 • Sci-Fi / Action • Dir. Matt Shakman

Plot Summary

Set in a vibrant, retro-futuristic 1960s universe, "Fantastic Four: First Steps" introduces Marvel's First Family—Reed Richards, Sue Storm, Johnny Storm, and Ben Grimm—as established heroes juggling their cosmic responsibilities with their tight-knit family dynamics.

Their world is threatened when the Silver Surfer arrives as a herald for the planet-devouring entity, Galactus. Facing an impossible choice when Galactus demands a sacrifice to spare their Earth, the team must unite like never before. With high-stakes cosmic battles and the fate of their world hanging in the balance, the Fantastic Four must prove that family is the greatest power of all.
