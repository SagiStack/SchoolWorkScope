Ted

2012 • Comedy / Fantasy • Dir. Seth MacFarlane

Plot Summary

John Bennett, a man whose childhood wish of bringing his teddy bear to life came true, now must decide between keeping the relationship with the bear, Ted, or his girlfriend, Lori. Ted, now a foul-mouthed, pot-smoking slacker, has been John's constant companion for decades.

As John tries to navigate adulthood and his relationship with Lori, Ted's antics and their bond threaten to derail his future. It's a hilarious and heartwarming story about friendship and growing up.
